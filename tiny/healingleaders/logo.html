Our logo...

Paying respect to our time dwelling in the Southwest... The shape and lines of the Healing Leaders logo reflect designs found on the "McElmo black-on-white" pottery made by Ancient Puebloans who lived in and around southwest Colorado roughly a thousand years ago. Its texture suggests a pictograph pecked into stone and flecked with colorful lichens.

The logo is designed as a three-circuit labyrinth with a spiral at the center. Whether walked or traced with a finger, following a labyrinth's path in to the center and out again has served since prehistoric times as a meditation when looking for answers to life's mysteries and problems, and the spiral is traditionally used to denote a spiritual quest.
